Ultimate Nasi Briyani

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Beef cubes - 500 g
  • Briyani Spice Mix
  • 1 table spoon of raisins and cashews

Directions

  1. cook the Beef cubes in the pressure cooker for 35minutes
  2. Add the rice and spices and cook in the pressure cooker for 10 minutes
  3. Top the end product with cashews and raisins and serve
