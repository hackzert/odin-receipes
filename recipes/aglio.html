Ultimate Aglio Olio

Ingredients

  • 1 bulb of Garlic
  • 300g of Pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of Salt
  • 1 table spoon of butter
  • 3 tablespoon of olive oil

Directions

  1. Chop the garlic finely and fry it under low flame for 5 minutes
  2. Cook the Pasta
  3. Mix the garlic with the pasta and butter and serve
